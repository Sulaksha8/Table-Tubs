Table Tubs

What are Table tubs?

Table Tubs are slidable drawers that belong underneath your table. Table Tubs are a practical and efficient storage. Instead of wasting your time walking to you normal tubs, everything you need is right under you. These drawers are designed to maximize space while providing easy access to items that are frequently used or needed. Table tubs can hold stationery, books and even rubbish. Overall, they add functionality, help maintain organization, and contribute to a clutter-free environment.
